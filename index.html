Introduction to IT and ITES industry

1. Introduction to ICT

✨Definition:

ICT (Information and Communication Technologies) are tools
for creating, storing, managing, and communicating digital
information,revolutionizing how we interact.

✨ IT vs. ITeS Industry:

Information Technology (IT): Using computers and software
to manage data (store, retrieve, manipulate).
IT Enabled Services (ITeS): Services provided remotely using
IT, like BPO (Business Process Outsourcing), KPO (Knowledge
Process Outsourcing).

✨ Importance & Applications of ICT:

➡️Communication: Instant global messaging via social media, apps, email.
➡️Education: E-learning, online resources, digital classrooms.
➡️Healthcare: Computerized diagnostics (CAT scans), patient monitoring.
➡️Business/Marketing: Online ads, performance tracking, e-commerce.
➡️Government (E-Governance): Online services, public safety.
➡️Economy: Drives economic growth and creates jobs.
